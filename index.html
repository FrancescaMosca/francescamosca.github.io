Hello!

I am currently a final year PhD Candidate in Artificial Intelligence working under the supervision of Dr. Jose M. Such and Prof. Peter McBurney at King's College London (KCL).
At KCL, I am part of the Cybersecurity and the Distributed AI Research Groups in the Department of Informatics.

I hold a Master in Intelligent Systems (2016, King's College London), a Master in Mathematical Engineering (2015, Politecnico di Torino, Italy) and a Bachelor in Applied Maths (2013, Politecnico di Torino, Italy).

My research interests lie at the intersection of Agent-based Modelling, Explainable AI, Human-Computer Interaction, Computational Social Choice, and Privacy. As an AI researcher, I aim to tackle real-world problems by developing ethical and sustainable AI applications.
